THE CONISTON ROUND AT CONISTON, ENGLAND

“There is no such thing
as bad weather, only
inappropraite clothing”

The Coniston Round in England known for it’s rugged mountains and is a difficult 7.9 kilometer loop hike located near Coniston, UK. During the Hike, visitors have the opprtunity to climb the famous peak the Old Man of Coniston. Save this hike for a long summer day.
