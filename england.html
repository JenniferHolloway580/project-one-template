THE CONISTON ROUND AT CONISTON, ENGLAND

“In all things of nature there
is something of the marvelous”

- Aristotle


The Coniston Round in England known for its rugged mountains is a difficult 7.9-kilometer loop trail located near Coniston, UK. During the Hike, visitors have the opportunity to climb the famous peak of the Old Man of Coniston. From the summit, there are excellent views of the surrounding hills. Other points of interest are the copper mines that date back to the 1500s located in the lake district. This is a hike that tourists and hiking enthusiasts will want to save for a long summer day.
