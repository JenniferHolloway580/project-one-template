HARDANGERVIDDA TRANSVERSE – NORWAY

“Only one who wanders
finds new paths”

- Norwegian proverb


Hardangervidda Transverse is Norways largest and most diverse national park. Whether you are looking for a place to hike, beautiful wilderness to explore, or fish Hardangervidda Transverse has it all. Enencompassing nearly 10,000 square kilometers of varied Arctic wilderness and biger than Yellowstone National Park in the U.S. During your hike, you willl encounter Northern Europe’s largest mountain plateau, the Vøringsfossen waterfall, Måbødalen canyon, and the majestic fjord landscape
