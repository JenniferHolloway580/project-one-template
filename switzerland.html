HAUTE ROUTE, SWITZERLAND

“There are no shortcuts
to any pace worth going”

- Helen Keller


The Haute Route in Switzerland is a spectacular difficult hike walking from the French to the Swiss Alps. Chamonix, France to Zermatt (Switzerland) in 10 to 12 days. This is the world of glaciers and snow-capped mountain peaks. The Haute Route is also known for its green valleys and flower-covered meadows. For hikers, the Haute Route has a short season lasting less than three months. June and July are the quietest months and most beautiful, but be aware of old snow on trails, that could potentially be dangerous.
